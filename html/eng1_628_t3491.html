1번 다음을 읽고, 글의 내용과 일치하는 것을 고르세요.

Hello, Mr. Smith,
My summer was great. Mr. and Mrs. Semple invited me to their farm. I had fun at the farm. Every morning, I picked up eggs with Mr. Semple. Mrs. Semple and I made butter, too. I also went camping with them.
How was your summer, Mr. Smith?
Did you stay in New York?
Please, write a letter to me.
From Judy.
