<Walking Tour of Paris>

How about a walking tour of Paris?
First, visit the Louvre Museum. You can see many great paintings there. Start from the bus stop. Go straight and turn left at the corner.
Second, visit the Eiffel Tower. You can see the Seine River from the tower. Start from the ABC library. Go straight two blocks and turn right. It’s on your right.
Enjoy your tour.
