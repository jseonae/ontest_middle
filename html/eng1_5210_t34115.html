The Little Prince

The little prince lives on a small star.
He has short curly hair. He’s wearing a long coat and green pants. He waters the flowers every day.
The little prince has a friend, Charlie. He has big ears and short legs. He has black eyes and a long tail, too.
Charlie and the little prince are good friends.
