Bear story

I’m Emily. I live in Alaska.
I love bears. I read books about them in my free time. I look for bears with my dad and take pictures.
But we can’t see many bears these days. It’s getting warm and the snow is melting.
The bears can’t find food. So they can’t live here.
How can we help them?
